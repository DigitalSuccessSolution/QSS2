Who We Are

Quick Support Solutions Built on Trust, Driven by Insight

Quick Support Solutions is a compliance-first audit & assurance firm that delivers precision-driven solutions across internal audit, retail audits, inventory & asset audits, manpower outsourcing, and background verification.

Originally established in 2015 as Quick Support Associate, the firm became an LLP in 2019 and was formally incorporated as QSS Audit & Assurance Services Pvt. Ltd. in 2025. Over the years, QSS has grown into a trusted name for scalable audit services across industries including retail, infrastructure, manufacturing, logistics, and hospitality.

Our strength lies in combining regulatory expertise with tech-enabled execution and operational control. With ISO certification, proprietary technology, and a nationwide delivery network, QSS empowers organizations seeking governance, control, and transparency.

Our unique 360° approach—Performance, Protection & Proof Assurance—ensures every business function is not just compliant, but audit-resilient, secure, and trust-enabled.

Protection

Proof

Validating trust through verifications, background checks, and digital authentication via QuickEx™. We provide the evidence needed for confident decision-making.
