Meet Our Chartered Accountants

Our team of expert Chartered Accountants brings years of experience, precision, and dedication to ensure your business thrives with strategic financial solutions.

Mr. JEEVAN SINGH (Managing Director)

A pioneer in the field of security with vast experience of many years. He launched his own Security Service Company and extended his wings with the motive of "sky is the limit". He has also diversified into Real Estate, Hotels, and Leasing Business. With his vision, dexterity, ingrained business acumen, and professionalism, he has managed and developed the security company to the top level. The company today has a national presence with 22 branches all over India and a guard force of over 6000 personnel.

Mr. RANJEET SINGH CHANDRAWAT (Executive Director)

A Management graduate with professional experience spread across admin, material management, business development, and client servicing in industries such as textile, FMCG, steel, and service industry. He has been instrumental in establishing business networks and developing training programs for specific manpower categories. He has organized management development programs for senior management in various reputed organizations.

Mr. DEEPNARAYAN DWIVEDI (General Manager)

An officer from Combat Arm of the Indian Army, with two and a half decades in Army service besides four years in the National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy. He has had long tenures in insurgent areas and served with the National Security Guard, India’s premier security setup. He has completed security courses with the Military Intelligence and Security College and Industrial Security under the aegis of DGR. He has handled VIP security at both national and international levels and coordinated with 18 standalone training venues of the Commonwealth Games where security was of utmost priority.
